DormPal

Your trusted ally in Dormitory Management

Project Type

Web System

Technologies Used

HTML, CSS, PHP, Javascript, APIs

Brief Overview

DormPal is a dormitory management system that helps admins efficiently manage boarders, assign room pricing, and track payments. It provides real-time monitoring of due and overdue payments, ensuring smooth financial tracking. With automated SMS notifications, boarders receive instant payment confirmations for added convenience.

Key Features

  • Boarder Management
  • Payment & Transaction Tracking
  • Due & Overdue Payment Alerts
  • SMS Payment Confirmation

Screenshots

Challenges & Solutions

Managing real-time payment updates and overdue notifications was challenging, but integrating an automated tracking system improved accuracy and efficiency.

Outcome & Impact

DormPal helps admins manage boarders and payments smoothly, making dorm operations more convenient.
