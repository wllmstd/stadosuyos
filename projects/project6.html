RouteEase

Redefining your daily commute.

Project Type

Prototype

Technologies Used

Figma, Canva

Brief Overview

RouteEase is a commuter-friendly prototype designed to simplify daily travel by providing real-time traffic updates and nearby transportation booking. It features an AI chat assistant, an AR travel view, and a recent location tracker to enhance the commuting experience. With RouteEase, users can navigate efficiently and make informed travel decisions.

Key Features

  • Real-Time Traffic Updates
  • Transportation Booking & Vehicle Info
  • AI Chat Assistance
  • Augmented Reality (AR) Travel View
  • Recent Location History

Screenshots

Challenges & Solutions

Simulating real-time traffic updates was challenging, but using mock data helped demonstrate the concept effectively.

Outcome & Impact

RouteEase showcases an innovative approach to improving daily commuting, highlighting the potential for smarter and more efficient travel solutions.
